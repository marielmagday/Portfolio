VIDEO GAME DESIGN


Description:

Our group was tasked to create an educational video game. We decided to create a math game for 7+. In this game, players will be able to:
- Be able to solve an equation using different methods
- Earns coins in order to purchase different cosmetics in order to make their game unique to themselves



Design and Production Process:

1. Thought of different games that could be fun for all ages
2. Researched into games that are well known
3. Created pixelated mockups of the game



Reflection:

After doing this project, I learned about:

- The importance of collaborating
- Researching different games that engage people of various age groups


If I could do things differently, I would:

- Research a different type of game to do

- Experiment different ways to create a game
